San Jose

San Jose is a large city surrounded by rolling hills in Silicon Valley, a major technology hub in California's Bay Area. Despite its large size, San Jose is the third safest large city in America with low crime rates and low criminal activity.

It is illegal for grocery stores in San Jose to use plastic bags. The 30 foot tall Chuck E. Cheese statue in San Jose is the world’s largest rat. San Jose recycles the most among large American cities.
